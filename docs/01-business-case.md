# Business Case

## Project

Sua Hair Studio — Booking & Management Platform

## Status

In Development

## Background

Sua Hair Studio is a Korean hair salon based in Melbourne, VIC with 5 active stylists across director, senior, and junior tiers. Prior to this project, all appointment booking was managed entirely through phone calls and SMS messages. No digital tooling of any kind existed for either customers or the salon owner.

## Problem Statement

The existing manual process created the following business problems:

| Problem                                                | Business Impact                                                                                                                                  |
| ------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Double bookings when two clients called simultaneously | Client dissatisfaction, lost revenue, reputational damage                                                                                        |
| No visibility across 5 stylists in real time           | Owner unable to manage floor without physical presence                                                                                           |
| No client history or loyalty tracking                  | Missed retention and upsell opportunities                                                                                                        |
| Bookings only possible during business hours           | Revenue lost outside 10am–6pm window                                                                                                             |
| Manual scheduling ignores service rest periods         | Stylist capacity underutilised — colour treatments block a stylist for the full duration when they could take another client during setting time |
| Service prices managed informally                      | No audit trail when prices change; historical revenue data inaccurate                                                                            |

## Goals

1. Allow customers to book appointments 24/7 without calling
2. Give the owner real-time visibility across all 5 stylists on a single screen
3. Prevent double bookings automatically via scheduling engine
4. Capture client history and enable loyalty recognition
5. Enable the owner to manage stylists, services, and pricing without code changes
6. Preserve historical booking accuracy when prices or stylists change

## Success Metrics

- Zero double bookings post-launch
- Owner can confirm/cancel/create bookings from a mobile device
- Customer can complete a booking in under 3 minutes
- Owner can add a new service or change a price without developer involvement

## Scope

**In scope (v1):**

- Online customer booking flow
- Admin dashboard with real-time timeline, booking management, client CRM, training tracking, analytics, and manage tab

**Out of scope (v1):**

- SMS/email confirmation
- Payment processing
- Customer accounts or login
- Multi-location support
- Staff payroll or commission tracking
